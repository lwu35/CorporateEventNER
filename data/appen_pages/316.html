      Our Profile

      Murphy Oil Corporation is an independent exploration and production company with a strong, oil-weighted portfolio of global offshore and onshore assets with upside to our exploration program. Our global offshore operations are complimentary to our North American onshore business. Exploration activities are focused in four main regions: U.S. and Mexico deepwater Gulf of Mexico, Brazil, Vietnam and Australia.
