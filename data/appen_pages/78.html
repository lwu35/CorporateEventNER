Corporate Profile

Four Corners Property Trust is an independent, publicly-traded company that owns, acquires and leases restaurant and other retail properties on a net basis. Our primary goal is to create long-term shareholder value through the payment of consistent and growing cash dividends and the growth of our cash flow and asset base. To achieve this goal, we intend to pursue a business strategy focused on opportunistic acquisitions and asset and tenant diversification.
