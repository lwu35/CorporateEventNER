Investor Overview

Corporate Profile

Chiasma develops and commercializes oral therapies utilizing its proprietary Transient Permeability Enhancer (TPE®) technology to reduce the burden of chronic injections for people with rare diseases. Chiasma’s proprietary TPE® technology is designed to convert select peptide based injectables into oral formulations. By leveraging this technology, patients may be alleviated of the burdens associated with chronic injectable therapies.

Using its TPE® technology, Chiasma developed MYCAPSSA® (octreotide), a somatostatin analog indicated for long-term maintenance treatment in acromegaly patients who have responded to and tolerated treatment with octreotide or lanreotide.

Chiasma is headquartered in the United States with a wholly-owned subsidiary in Israel.
